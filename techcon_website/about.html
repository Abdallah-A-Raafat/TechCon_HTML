History

TechCon was founded in 2010 in Silicon Valley as a grassroots initiative to bring together tech enthusiasts and innovators. It has grown into a premier global conference, hosting thousands annually and featuring groundbreaking technology discussions.

Over the years, TechCon has expanded to include diverse tracks, from AI to sustainability, shaping the future of the tech industry.

Mission

TechCon 2024 is dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and inclusivity in the global tech community. Our mission is to provide a platform for sharing knowledge and inspiring solutions to pressing challenges.

We strive to empower attendees through thought-provoking sessions and networking opportunities.
