Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, hosting over 10,000 attendees annually. Key milestones include our first international event in 2015 and the introduction of virtual attendance in 2020.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to bridge the gap between cutting-edge technology and real-world applications. We aim to:

  • Foster collaboration across industries
  • Democratize access to tech education
  • Showcase groundbreaking innovations
  • Inspire the next generation of technologists
